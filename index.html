Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption process?

The process starts with an application, a meet-and-greet, and a home visit to ensure a good match.

Are all dogs vaccinated?

Yes, all dogs are fully vaccinated, microchipped, and have a recent health check.

Can I return a dog if it doesnâ€™t work out?

We understand that sometimes situations change. We accept returns if it's not the right fit, with our team assisting in the transition.

Are there adoption fees?

Yes, adoption fees help cover medical, training, and care expenses. Fees vary by age and breed but are listed with each dog.

Do you provide post-adoption support?

Yes, we offer post-adoption support, including training tips, behavioral advice, and health resources to help with the adjustment period.
